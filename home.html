Moon Landing - 1969

On July 20, 1969, Apollo 11 successfully landed the first humans on the Moon. Neil Armstrong became the first person to walk on the Moon, saying the famous words: “That's one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind.”

Invention of the Internet - 1983

The modern internet was born in 1983 when ARPANET adopted the TCP/IP protocol. This laid the foundation for the global network we rely on today.

Public Launch of ChatGPT - In November 2022

OpenAI released ChatGPT to the public, revolutionizing how people interact with artificial intelligence. The chatbot quickly gained global attention for its ability to generate human-like responses and assist with a wide range of tasks.

Tuwaiq Academy Launch - 2019

Tuwaiq Academy was launched in 2019 as one of Saudi Arabia’s leading technology and programming academies. It offers intensive bootcamps and training programs in areas like cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and software development, empowering thousands of youth to become future tech leaders in the Kingdom.

Riyadh Season Opening - In October 2019

Saudi Arabia launched the first Riyadh Season, a large-scale entertainment and cultural festival. The event featured concerts, international shows, sports, and art exhibitions, attracting millions of visitors and showcasing a new era of tourism and entertainment in the Kingdom.

Launch of the First iPhone - In January 2007

Apple introduced the first iPhone, revolutionizing the mobile phone industry. With its touchscreen interface and internet capabilities, the iPhone marked the beginning of the modern smartphone era.
